Hi, I'm Purnima Arora

Born in New Delhi, India, I started my career as a professional artist a few years back. My work is an exploration of numerous detailed and delicate lines, drawn layer by layer until they form their true sense. 
Working with pencils, acrylics, and oils, I employ a bold colour palette and fluid style to give my art work a presence that captures the essence of a vivid beautiful world.
So far in career I've been awarded and honoured on several occasions that bought me the much needed recognition besides becoming the driving force to further ignite my passion to elevate my levels of perfection.
